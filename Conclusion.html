After analyzing machine learning models and graphs, visualizing the data with Tableau, we can classify that forests of Brazil are shrinking. Another linear regression displayed with its graph that since 1990 till 2020, the deforestation trend remains the same -- deforestation is increasing, the area squared kilometers covered with trees is decreasing.
